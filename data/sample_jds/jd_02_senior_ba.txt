Senior Business Analyst — AI Products
Location: Remote (EU timezones)

Company Overview
We're building AI-powered tools for enterprise knowledge workers. Our culture is
collaborative, fast-paced, and deeply technical — but we also value extensive
documentation and process rigor across all teams.

Role Summary
We're seeking a Senior Business Analyst to bridge the gap between our AI/ML
engineering team and business stakeholders. You'll be the go-to person for
requirements gathering, process mapping, and translating technical capabilities
into business value.

Responsibilities
- Lead requirements-gathering workshops with cross-functional stakeholders
- Map current-state and future-state business processes
- Translate AI/ML model capabilities and limitations into business-friendly language
- Define acceptance criteria for new features in collaboration with engineering
- Build and maintain dashboards to track key business metrics
- Support change management initiatives as new AI features roll out

Requirements
- 5+ years of Business Analyst or related experience
- Strong stakeholder management and facilitation skills
- Hands-on experience with SQL for data analysis
- Demonstrated ability to work with technical teams (engineering, data science)
- Excellent documentation skills — BRDs, process maps, user stories
- Experience working in a startup or fast-paced environment

Preferred Qualifications
- Experience in EdTech, FinTech, or other regulated/complex domains
- Exposure to AI/ML product development lifecycle
- Six Sigma, CBAP, or similar BA certification
- MBA or equivalent advanced degree

This is a junior-to-mid pay band role, but we need someone who can hit the
ground running with senior-level independence and minimal oversight from day one.
