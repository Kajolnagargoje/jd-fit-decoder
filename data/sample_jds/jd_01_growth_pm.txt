Product Manager — Growth (EdTech SaaS)
Location: Dublin, Ireland (Hybrid, 3 days/week in office)

About Us
We're a fast-growing EdTech SaaS platform serving 2M+ learners across Europe.
We're looking for a Product Manager to join our Growth team and own the
onboarding and activation experience.

What You'll Do
- Own the product roadmap for onboarding, activation, and early retention
- Define and track success metrics (activation rate, time-to-value, D7/D30 retention)
- Run A/B tests and translate results into prioritized backlog items
- Partner closely with Engineering, Design, and Data teams
- Write clear PRDs and user stories with well-defined acceptance criteria
- Present roadmap and results to senior leadership monthly

What We're Looking For
- 3+ years of product management experience, ideally in SaaS or EdTech
- Strong analytical skills; comfortable working with SQL and dashboarding tools
- Proven track record of running experiments (A/B testing) and driving measurable
  improvements in activation or retention metrics
- Excellent written and verbal communication skills
- Experience with Agile/Scrum methodologies; CSPO or similar certification a plus
- Bachelor's degree required; Master's in a quantitative field is a bonus
- Must be eligible to work in Ireland without sponsorship

Nice to Have
- Experience with tools like Amplitude, Mixpanel, or similar product analytics platforms
- Background in B2C subscription products
- Familiarity with AI/ML-powered personalization features

We move fast and wear many hats — you'll need to be comfortable with ambiguity
while also bringing rigorous process and structure to a scaling team.
